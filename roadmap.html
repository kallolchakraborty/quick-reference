🧭 ML Study Roadmap

A guided study plan from absolute beginner to Staff+ FAANG ML engineer. Choose your track based on your background and timeline. Every milestone links directly to the relevant content in the Quick Bytes documentation.

🚀 3-Month Crash Track

For engineers who already have ML experience and need to focus on FAANG interview preparation. ~20 hours/week.

Month 1: Interview Fundamentals

  • • ML Fundamentals — bias-variance, metrics, CV, data leakage
  • • Supervised Learning — linear/logistic regression, trees, XGBoost, SVM
  • • Chestnut Questions — 30 Q&As, (sections 1-20: core, 21-30: Staff+)
  • • Behavioral (STAR) — 13 scenarios (sections 1-8: core, 9-13: Staff+)

Month 2: Deep Learning & System Design

  • • NN Training + Types of ANNs + Activation Functions
  • • Zero to Hero — micrograd, BPE, nanoGPT, KV cache
  • • ML System Design — 6 full designs + deployment/monitoring
  • • Rec Systems — CF, MF, two-tower, multi-stage

Month 3: Staff+ Deep Dive & Mock Interviews

  • • Staff+ System Design — ML platform, real-time, multi-stage ranking, A/B platform
  • • LLM Engineering — RAG, fine-tuning, RLHF, agents, serving
  • • Production ML Ops — online learning, feature platform, cascades
  • • Mock Interview Walkthroughs — full transcripts with scoring rubrics
  • • Leadership & Strategy — org-wide influence, technical vision, incident response
  • • Company Focus — Staff+ round structure per company

🌱 12-Month Deep Track

For true beginners. ~8-10 hours/week. Assumes no ML or advanced math background.

Month 1-2: Math Foundations

  • • Learn Python basics (if not already)
  • • ML Math — probability, linear algebra, calculus, statistics
  • • Linear algebra essentials: vectors, matrices, eigenvalues, SVD
  • • Probability: Bayes' theorem, distributions, MLE, KL divergence
  • • Calculus: partial derivatives, chain rule, gradient descent
  • • Statistics: hypothesis testing, p-values, confidence intervals

Month 3-4: Classical ML Foundations

  • • ML Fundamentals
  • • Supervised Learning
  • • Unsupervised Learning
  • • Data-Centric AI
  • • A/B Testing

Month 5-7: Deep Learning

  • • Intro to Neural Networks
  • • Activation Functions
  • • NN Training
  • • Types of ANNs
  • • Zero to Hero — build everything from scratch
  • • Coding Patterns

Month 8-9: System Design & Advanced Topics

  • • ML System Design
  • • Rec Systems
  • • GPU Deep Dive
  • • ML Project Structure
  • • NLP Deep Dive
  • • Computer Vision
  • • RL & RLHF

Month 10-11: Staff+ & Advanced

  • • LLM Engineering
  • • Staff+ System Design
  • • Production ML Ops
  • • Interpretability & Fairness
  • • Leadership & Strategy
  • • Research Papers

Month 12: Interview Prep & Mock Interviews

  • • Mock Interview Walkthroughs

  • • Schedule real mock interviews with peers
